Burger Recipe

Description

This recipe is showing you how to make a cheeseburger. Which is my favorite meal.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Cheese
  • Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Place the patty on the bottom bun.
  2. Place the cheese on the patty.
  3. Place the lettuce and onions on top of the cheese.
  4. Place the top bun to finish the burger
